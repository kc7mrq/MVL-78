MISSION VALLEY LODGE #78

BROTHERLY LOVE

Every true Freemason will show tolerance and respect for the opinions of others and will behave with compassion and understanding to his fellows.
RELIEF

From earliest times Freemasons have been taught to help those in distress without detriment to their dependents, and to give their support to outside charities.
TRUTH

Freemasons strive for truth. Masonry requires high moral standards and its members endeavour to uphold these principles in their public and private lives.

1102 4 Ave. E. Polson MT 59860

Mission Valley Lodge meets every 1st and 3rd Wednesday at 7:30PM

If you are interested in joining the craft, we hope that you will find the information here helpful in answering your questions.

Mission Valley Lodge #78 is a Regular and Recognized Masonic lodge located in Polson, MT, having a charter through the

Grand Lodge of MT, AF&AM, which is recognized by the United Grand Lodge of England.

Mission Valley Lodge (MVL) goes dark from the second week of June through August 31st
